Cancellation and Refund Policy

At Ermunai Organic Farm Foods, we aim to ensure complete satisfaction for every order. However, due to the perishable nature of organic food products, our cancellation and refund policy is as follows.

1. Order Cancellations

Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement or before dispatch, whichever is earlier. Once dispatched, the order cannot be canceled.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds will only be provided for:

  • Products damaged during transit
  • Wrong items delivered
  • Non-delivery of order due to our fault

3. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after delivery
  • Opened or used products
  • Delayed delivery due to courier partner issues
