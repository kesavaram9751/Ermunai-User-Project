Celebrating a Milestone: My Journey with the All India Women Start-a-thon

By Ermunai Farm 6 Min Read

Dear Friends, Colleagues, and Well-Wishers,

I am thrilled to share a moment of pride and gratitude with all of you! Recently, I had the incredible opportunity to participate in Stage 2 of the All India Women Start-a-thon, a platform that empowers and celebrates the entrepreneurial spirit of women across India.

What the Experience Meant to Me

From the moment I submitted my video pitch, I knew this was more than just a competition — it was a chance to voice my ideas, share my passion, and stand among remarkable women innovators from diverse backgrounds. Crafting the video, articulating my vision, and stepping into the spotlight was both challenging and deeply rewarding.

Participating in this event helped me:

  • Reflect on my entrepreneurial goals
  • Gain confidence in sharing my ideas publicly
  • Connect with a community that supports women empowerment
  • Learn valuable insights about pitching and communication

Recognition That Inspires

I am genuinely honored to have received a Certificate of Recognition from the All India Women Start-a-thon team, in appreciation of my contribution at Stage 2. This acknowledgment means a lot to me — not just as a keepsake, but as validation of the hard work, creativity, and belief I put into my pitch.

Receiving this certificate has made me reflect on how far I’ve come — and how much I still aspire to achieve.

Gratitude to the Organizers

A heartfelt thank you to:

  • 🔹 All India Women Start-a-thon – for providing a national platform for women entrepreneurs
  • 🔹 BlackSoil – for powering the initiative
  • 🔹 Shooting Stars Foundation – for bringing it to life

Your support for women innovators is shaping dreams into reality. This recognition motivates me to dream bigger and work harder. I’m excited to continue my journey, take on new challenges, and contribute meaningfully to the world of innovation and entrepreneurship.

To all the emerging women leaders out there — dream boldly, pursue relentlessly, and celebrate every step forward.
