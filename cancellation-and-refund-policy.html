Cancellation and Refund Policy

At Ermunai Organic Farm Foods, we are committed to providing you with the highest quality organic food products. However, we understand that there may be instances where you need to cancel an order or request a refund. Please read the policy below to understand how cancellations and refunds work.

1. Order Cancellation

- You can cancel your order within 12 hours of placing it, provided it has not yet been processed or shipped.
- To cancel your order, please contact our support team at ermunaiorganicfarmfoods@gmail.com with your order details.
- Once your order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled.

2. Refund Policy

- Refunds are applicable only for orders cancelled within the allowed time or in case of defective/damaged products.
- For damaged or defective items, please share a photo or video proof with our team within 24 hours of delivery.
- After verification, we will initiate a refund or replacement as per your preference.

3. Mode of Refund

- Refunds will be processed to the same payment method used at checkout (e.g., UPI, Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking).
- Refunds may take 5–7 business days to reflect in your account, depending on your bank/payment provider.

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Products that have been opened, used, or tampered with after delivery.
  • Perishable items that are not returned within 24 hours of delivery.
  • Orders cancelled after shipping.
